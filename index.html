🎧 About Me

I'm a music composer and sound designer who thrives on crafting immersive audio experiences — whether it's scoring films, building game soundtracks, or engineering my own audio gear.

My journey started with the guitar, but I quickly fell in love with creating entire worlds through sound. These days, I'm deep into film scoring, game music, and experimenting with synths, field recordings, and foley. I also build my own MIDI controllers and audio tools to push my sound further.

Whether it's composing for visuals or designing hardware, I'm all about making music that sticks — music that makes people feel something. This site is a mix of the projects I’ve built, scored, and experimented with. Scroll through, explore, and hit me up if you want to collaborate.
